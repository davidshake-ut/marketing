Who this is (and isn't) for

We work best with a specific type of integrator. Being specific about who we serve means better outcomes for everyone.

✓ This is for you if…

✓You're a low-voltage integrator or an electrical contractor with a low-voltage division, doing $2M to $20M in revenue
✓You're making critical technology decisions without dedicated senior tech leadership
✓You have customers who trust you and could buy more, if you had more to sell
✓You want to expand your offering but can't afford the risk of doing it wrong
✓Ops and sales are maxed out and need a structured plan, not more chaos
✓You've lost bids because your solution set was too narrow vs. larger competitors

✗ This is NOT for you if…

✗You need a full-time internal employee. We're fractional and project-based.
✗You're a $100M+ enterprise with an existing CTO or tech leadership team
✗You're looking for a software development firm or IT managed services provider
✗You want a vendor to own the outcome. We equip your team to own execution.
✗You're not ready to commit to a structured process. Ad-hoc consulting isn't how we work.
